01. Company Profile

About RVNL

NEW DELHI, INDIA

Expediting Rail Infrastructure Development

Rail Vikas Nigam Limited (RVNL) is a premier Navratna Central Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under India's Ministry of Railways, established in 2003 to expedite rail infrastructure development. It acts as a project implementation agency for doubling, new lines, gauge conversion, and metro projects, with a strong, growing presence in international infrastructure projects.
