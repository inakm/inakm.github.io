01. Origin & Vision

About GatiGo

HYDERABAD, INDIA

@Safar Bhii, Bachatt Bhi!!

Carpooling for Smarter, Greener Cities

The Problem

Urban India is grappling with three interlinked crises: traffic congestion that adds hours to daily commutes, commuting costs that drain household budgets, and vehicle emissions accelerating urban pollution. In Hyderabad alone, over 3 million personal vehicles clog roads every day — yet most carry only one passenger.

The Origin Story

The idea for GatiGo was born at Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad. After a long flight, a founder noticed that cab prices were prohibitively high — and that most fellow passengers were traveling to nearby destinations. A spontaneous shared cab turned into an insight: there is a massive, unmet demand for structured, trusted carpooling in Indian cities.

What GatiGo Does

GatiGo is a tech-driven carpooling platform that connects drivers who have empty seats with commuters traveling in the same direction. Using a mobile app, users can find or offer a carpool in minutes. The platform covers both intra-city daily commutes and intercity travel.

Core Features

  • Smart Ride Matching — AI-powered matching of drivers and riders
  • Real-Time GPS Tracking — Live location sharing for safety
  • In-App SOS Alerts — Emergency button connected to contacts
  • Verified Profiles — Government ID and background checks
  • Flexible Scheduling — On-demand or recurring rides
  • Intercity Carpooling — Long-distance shared rides
  • Corporate Programs — White-label solutions for employees
  • Secure Digital Payments — UPI, cards, and wallet support

GatiGo's Unfair Advantages

  • Dedicated urban carpooling focus — not a feature bolted onto a ride-hailing app
  • Corporate B2B channel provides predictable, recurring revenue from Day 1
  • Superior safety stack: verified drivers, in-app SOS, live tracking
  • Eco-friendly positioning resonates with Hyderabad's growing green-conscious tech workforce
  • Hyderabad launch creates defensible local network effect before national scale

GatiGo is not just an app — it's an urban mobility movement.

We are addressing a ₹20 billion annual market with a proven model, clear revenue streams, a path to profitability by Year 3, and a team driven by purpose. With seed funding of ₹1.5–2 Cr, GatiGo will achieve product-market fit in Hyderabad, demonstrate scalability, and set the stage for a Series A expansion across India's top metro cities.
