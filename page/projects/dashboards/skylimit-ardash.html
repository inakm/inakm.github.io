How This Dashboard Works

What is Accounts Receivable (AR) Aging? AR aging categorizes unpaid invoices by how long they've been outstanding. It helps you identify which customers are paying on time and which ones need follow-up.

How to use this dashboard:

  1. Upload your Master Report from Tally ERP (Input → Master Report) to populate the ledger with all invoices.
  2. Upload Payment Logs (Input → Payment Logs) or log payments manually in the Payment Desk tab to track what's been collected.
  3. Upload a Calling List (Input → Calling List) to sync call statuses and remarks from your collection team.
  4. Use the Calling List tab to prioritize follow-ups on invoices overdue >30 days. Update call statuses after each interaction.
  5. Export reports (Output → CSV/PDF/Sync JSON) to share with stakeholders or archive daily snapshots.

Tips for better cash flow:

  • Focus on 90+ Day aging items first — these have the highest risk of becoming bad debt.
  • Use WhatsApp/Email buttons in the Calling List to send quick payment reminders.
  • Track the Today Collected KPI daily to measure collection team performance.
  • Log all payments promptly to keep the DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) metric accurate.
  • Export and backup your data via Sync JSON regularly — all data is stored only in your browser.

Data source: Tally ERP. All processing happens locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Your data stays on your device. All records — invoices, payments, call logs — are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB using the Tally ERP data you upload. Nothing is transmitted to any server, collected, or shared. You retain full control and may clear or export your data at any time.
