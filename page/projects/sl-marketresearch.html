KEY INSIGHTS & POSITIONING

Pricing Positioning

Stanza positioned as mid-to-premium vs budget competitors. Zolo's double occupancy at ₹8,520 vs Stanza's Salzburg double at ₹14,000 — ~64% price premium.

Demand Opportunity

Christ University alone has ~30,000 students with 60-66% outstation — representing a significant addressable market for co-living. Limited college hostel capacity amplifies the opportunity.

Competitive Edge

Stanza's differentiators: better security (CCTV+Guard), power backup, standardized food services, and professional housekeeping. Competitors lack consistency in security and power backup, giving Stanza a clear service edge.

Data Gaps

Missing: sanctioned seat counts, hostel occupancy figures, Stanza lock-in/notice terms, competitor AC pricing, and early-exit penalties. Priority areas for Phase 2 research.

Research Methodology & Disclaimer

This research was conducted for educational and assessment purposes as part of the Stanza Living Growth Associate evaluation. Data was collected through field visits, online research, student interviews, and security guard conversations. Confidence levels are self-assessed and clearly stated per data point.

Sensitive commercial data (pricing, contract terms, occupancy figures) are protected with confidential markers. Full access requires explicit authorization from Stanza Living.
